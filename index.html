The Problem

THE VPN PARADOX

Following a significant surge in VPN usage, the government began formal consultation into closing the loophole. Here's what's happening right now.

01

The Online Safety Act

Full implementation forces commercial VPNs to introduce age-verification and Know Your Customer protocols for UK users.

02

The Identity Trap

To buy privacy from your ISP, you must hand your passport, face scan, or bank details to a third-party VPN company. The "no-logs" promise becomes meaningless.

03

The Exit Ramp

Self-hosted VPNs operate outside this framework entirely. You're not a customer in a database. You're the administrator of your own private infrastructure.
